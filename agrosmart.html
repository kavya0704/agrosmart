Research & References

Our solution is grounded in extensive research and real-world data to ensure its effectiveness and credibility.

Academic & Technical Research

We have leveraged findings from studies on IoT in agriculture, soil science, and micro-controller-based automation systems to inform our design. Key topics include sensor accuracy in varied soil types and data-driven crop watering schedules.

Market & User Analysis

Our solution is a direct response to a gap identified in the market for affordable, accessible smart irrigation systems for small-scale farmers in developing regions. We have conducted interviews to understand their needs and challenges.

Technology Validation

Our approach has been validated through hands-on prototype testing and a pilot program. The success of our pilot farmer's experience serves as a strong case study for the system's practical benefits.
